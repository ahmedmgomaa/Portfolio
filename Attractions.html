Egyptian Attractions

The Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient structures located on the outskirts of Cairo,
Egypt. They are among the most iconic landmarks in the world and were built as
royal tombs for pharaohs during the Old Kingdom period.


The Sphinx

The Sphinx is a large limestone statue with a human head and a lion's body, located
near the pyramids of Giza. It is one of the most recognizable monuments in the world
and is believed to have been built during the reign of Pharaoh Khafre.
