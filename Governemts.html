The most popular Governments in Egypt


Alexandria

Alexandria, Top attractions in Alexandria, Egypt, blend ancient history with Mediterranean
charm, featuring the massive Bibliotheca Alexandrina, the historic Citadel of Qaitbay, and
the underground Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa. Visitors can explore the Roman Amphitheater,
stroll the Corniche waterfront, and visit the Alexandria National Museum.


Cairo

Cairo offers a blend of world-famous ancient wonders, medieval Islamic architecture, and
vibrant modern districts. For travelers in 2026, the newly fully-opened Grand Egyptian
Museum is the city's premier cultural destination.
